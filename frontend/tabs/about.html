About Us

TANUH (Translational Artificial Intelligence for Networked Universal Healthcare) is a Section 8 not-for-profit organization promoted by Indian Institute of Science (IISc). Funded by the Ministry of Education as an AI Centre of Excellence in Healthcare, TANUH is dedicated to advancing the Government of India's vision to "Make AI in India and Make AI Work for India."
