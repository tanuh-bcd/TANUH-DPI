Clinical Document

PDF to ABDM Conversion

Transform raw clinical documents into ABDM compliant FHIR JSON resources. Our pipeline handles unstructured discharge summaries, diagnostic reports, and prescriptions.

  • Instantly digitizes handwritten and printed clinical records
  • Eliminates manual data entry for hospitals and clinics
  • LangGraph orchestrated extraction outputting validated FHIR R4 JSON bundles
Insurance Policy

PDF to NHCX Conversion

Process insurance policies and claim documents into strictly validated National Health Claims Exchange (NHCX) JSON formats.

  • Dramatically accelerates health insurance claim approvals
  • Automatically reads complex policy terms, sub-limits, and waiting periods
  • Dependency-aware topological extraction to FHIR R4 NHCX schemas
Privacy Filter

Intelligent Privacy Filter

De-identify medical documents using AI-powered PII detection. Ensure full compliance with the DPDP Act with our zero-retention architecture.

  • Protects patient confidentiality by automatically hiding sensitive details
  • Ensures full compliance with national data privacy laws (DPDP Act)
  • 3-Layer AI Architecture combining NER models and Regex patterns
Forgery Detection

Forgensic: Document Forgery Detection

Conduct classical forensic analysis to detect digital manipulation, splicing, and tampering in clinical documents and claims forms.

  • Automatically flags manipulated or fraudulent medical documents
  • Secures the health insurance ecosystem against false claims
  • Applies Error Level Analysis (ELA) and deep metadata inspection in under 2 seconds
